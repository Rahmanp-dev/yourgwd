# Orbit Arena — Sponsor Pitch

---

## What Is Orbit Arena?

**India's first live sales hackathon.** 80–100 aspiring sales professionals, developers, and project managers compete in teams for 48 hours to close real business deals using AI-powered tools. Real leads. Real conversations. Real payments.

## Why Sponsor Orbit Arena?

### Your Brand Reaches:
- **80–100 ambitious participants** — future entrepreneurs, sales leaders, and tech talent
- **400–500 families** via social media content generated during the event
- **10,000+ impressions** from pre-event, live, and post-event content across Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter

### Tangible Outcomes:
- Every deal closed during Arena uses **your payment gateway / CRM / platform**

- "Orbit Arena powered by [Your Brand]" on ALL materials
- Logo on: banners, certificates, team kits, leaderboard screen, social media posts
- 5-minute keynote slot during Opening Ceremony
- Booth/desk at the venue for the full event
- Access to top 10 performer profiles for recruitment
- Product integration during the event (e.g., all payments through your gateway, all teams use your CRM)
- Post-event case study co-branded with your logo

### 🥈 Associate Sponsor — ₹25,000 (3 Available)

- Access to participant database (with consent)
- 1 social media post tagging your brand
- Category exclusivity (e.g., "Payment Partner", "CRM Partner", "Cloud Partner")

### 🥉 Community Sponsor — ₹10,000 (5 Available)

- Logo on event banners and certificates

- Flyer distribution at the venue
