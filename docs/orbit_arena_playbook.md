# GWD ORBIT — Arena Playbook v1.0

> The complete operational guide for running Orbit Arena — India's First Live Sales Hackathon.

---

## Event Identity

**Name:** Orbit Arena
**Tagline:** "48 Hours. Real Leads. Real Deals. Real Money."
**Format:** 48-hour team competition
**Capacity:** 80–100 participants (10 teams of 5–8)
**Frequency:** Quarterly (4 events/year)

---

## Scoring System

### Point Values

| Action | Points | Verification |
|---|---|---|
| Lead contacted (first message sent with screenshot) | 1 | Screenshot in team channel |
| Meaningful conversation (2+ replies exchanged) | 3 | Screenshot |
| Discovery call completed (5+ min voice/video) | 8 | Call log or recording |
| Live demo/preview shared | 10 | Link sent + client response |
| Proposal sent (with pricing) | 15 | Proposal PDF + delivery proof |
| Meeting booked for post-Arena follow-up | 20 | Calendar invite screenshot |
| Deal closed — verbal agreement | 30 | Voice note or message confirmation |
| Deal closed — payment received | 50 | Razorpay payment confirmation |
| Retainer agreement signed | 25 (bonus) | Signed document |

### Leaderboard Categories

1. **Team Total Score** — Sum of all team members' points
2. **Top Individual Closer** — Highest individual score
3. **Best Revenue Generated** — Total ₹ in confirmed payments
4. **Best Pitch** — Audience vote during closing ceremony
5. **Best Content** — Most engaging behind-the-scenes content created during Arena

---

## Team Structure

Each team of 5 must include:

| Role | Responsibility During Arena |
|---|---|
| **Deal Architect (DA)** | Contacts leads, has conversations, closes deals |
| **Project Manager (PM)** | Scopes work for interested clients, creates proposals |
| **Developer** | Generates live preview pages, answers technical questions |
| **Designer** | Creates quick mockups, social media assets for pitches |
| **Wildcard** | Research, content creation, support — fills any gap |

Teams are formed during the Opening Ceremony. Participants indicate their preferred role during registration. GWD ensures balanced teams.

---

## Lead Distribution

- Each team receives **50 pre-qualified leads** from GWD's AI pipeline
- Leads are distributed evenly across niches:
  - 10 dental clinics / medical practices
  - 10 restaurants / cafes
  - 10 fashion boutiques / retail stores
  - 10 fitness trainers / gyms
  - 10 interior designers / architects
- All leads are real businesses with verified contact info
- Leads are exclusive to each team — no overlap

## Anti-Cheating Rules

1. All client conversations must be documented with screenshots
2. Payments must go through team-specific Razorpay links (no cash deals)
3. Teams cannot contact leads assigned to other teams
4. No pre-existing client relationships count (all leads are new)
5. AI tools are allowed and encouraged (that's the whole point)
6. Any fabricated evidence = immediate disqualification + lifetime Orbit ban

---

## Post-Arena Pipeline

### For Deals Closed During Arena
- Winning teams continue to deliver the projects (first real Orbit work)
- GWD provides PM support for Arena-originated projects
- Standard Orbit commission split applies

### For Leads In Pipeline (Not Yet Closed)
- Teams keep their lead lists post-Arena
- Any deal closed within 30 days of Arena counts toward their Orbit track record
- DAs who convert pipeline leads post-Arena earn full DA commission

### For Participants Not Selected as Founding Operators
- All participants get Orbit Member access (Tier 0)
- They can apply for Batch 2 (Month 3)
- Arena participation is noted on their Orbit profile (permanent credential)
