The Intelligence Play

How to Find Them via Instagram

This is not Google Maps. This requires crawling directly inside Instagram to find people who are actively selling but have no website in their bio.

01

Hashtag Crawling

Target niche hashtags like #oxidisedjewellery or #boutiquestore. Pull the top 100 posts. Extract the profile URL of each account that posted.

02

Bio Analysis: No Website Filter

Visit each profile. If their bio link is a Linktree, WhatsApp, or Facebook — not a real domain — they qualify as a lead. Simple automated check.

03

Contact Extraction

Pull WhatsApp number from bio text. Cross-reference with Google: site:instagram.com "[brand name]" email to find business contact details.

04

Email Finding via Hunter.io

Run brand name and city through Hunter.io or Apollo.io API to find professional emails. Append to the MongoDB lead record automatically.

CRM Upgrades Needed

What to Add to the System

To fully automate this Instagram pipeline, the GWD CRM needs these new features built into it.

Instagram Lead Importer

Takes a hashtag as input and outputs Instagram profiles with no website in bio, along with their WhatsApp number and follower count.

Email Outreach Module

A new Email tab in each lead CRM profile. Pre-populated with personalised message and their Vercel preview link. One-click send via SMTP or SendGrid.

E-Commerce Preview Builder

Instead of a landing page, generate a Shopify-style preview with product grid, cart, and checkout flow customized to the niche and brand colours from Instagram.

Email Hunter Integration

Run each Instagram brand through Hunter.io or Apollo.io API to auto-find professional emails and append them to the MongoDB lead record.

WhatsApp and Email Status Tracker

Track whether the lead was contacted via WhatsApp or Email, when it was sent, and whether they opened the preview link via UTM tracking.

Lead Scoring Engine

Auto-score each lead by follower count, engagement rate, posting frequency, and whether they actively sell in posts. Prioritise warmest leads first.
