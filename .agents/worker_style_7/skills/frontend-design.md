# Frontend Design Methodology (Local Copy)
Source: d:\GWD\Sales Machine\.agents\skills\frontend-design\SKILL.md

Approach this as the design lead at a small studio known for giving every client a visual identity that could not be mistaken for anyone else's.

## Design principles
1. Ground it in the subject.
2. The hero is a thesis.
3. Typography carries the personality.
4. Structure is information.
5. Leverage motion deliberately.
6. Match complexity to the vision.
7. Words are design material.

## Design System exploration
- Color: 4-6 named hex values.
- Type: 2+ roles (display, body, utility).
- Layout: ASCII wireframe and layout concept.
- Signature: a single unique element.
