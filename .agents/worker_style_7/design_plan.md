# Design Plan - Meena Jewellers & Musaddilal Jewellers

This plan outlines the design tokens, layout wireframes, and unique signature elements for both stores under the Soft Pastel Luxury theme.

---

## 1. Meena Jewellers (meena-jewellers-banjara-hills)
**Theme**: Royal Nizam Heirloom & Feminine Delicate. Classic, ornate, centered, traditional borders, gold details.

### Design Tokens
- **Base Background**: Cream Blush (`#FFFBFB`)
- **Accents**: Soft Rose Accent (`#FFEBEB`), Blush Pink (`#FADBD8`)
- **Metal / Border Accent**: Deep Rose Gold (`#B88A7D`), Bright Gold (`#D4A373`)
- **Panel / Card Background**: Ivory Panel (`#FFFDF6` with 1px border)
- **Text Color**: Deep Charcoal-Brown (`#2C2220`)

### Typography
- **Display**: `Prata` (Google Font - elegant modern serif with teardrop terminals)
- **Body**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` (sans-serif - clean and friendly)

### Layout Concept
Centered classic alignment, delicate rules, and ornate cards.

### Signature Element
**Heirloom Jewelry Customizer**: An ornate, traditional vector display of a classic Nizam necklace which dynamically changes color (Gold/Rose Gold/Platinum) and gemstone glows (Ruby/Emerald/Sapphire/Pearl) on selection.

## 2. Musaddilal Jewellers (musaddilal-jewellers-basheer-bagh)
**Theme**: Modern Avant-Garde Luxury. Asymmetrical layout, sleek double borders, minimal hairline frames, high fashion.

### Design Tokens
- **Base Background**: Soft Ivory Rose (`#FAF6F5`)
- **Accents**: Blush Mist (`#F2E6E6`), Deep Blush Accent (`#C39A9B`)
- **Metal / Border Accent**: Warm Rose Gold (`#C89D95`), Modern Platinum (`#E2E8F0`)
- **Panel / Card Background**: Crisp White Panel (`#FFFFFF`)
- **Text Color**: Velvet Black-Brown (`#221E1D`)

### Typography
- **Display**: `Cinzel` (Google Font - prestigious Roman-style serif)
- **Body**: `Inter` (sans-serif - clean, precise)

### Layout Concept
Asymmetrical editorial columns, floating offset panels, thin clean rules.
