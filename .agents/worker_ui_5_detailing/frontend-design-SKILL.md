# Frontend Design Skill Summary

- Match design to subject's world, materials, and vernacular.
- Hero should be a thesis (most characteristic thing first).
- Typography carries personality: pair display and body faces intentionally.
- Structure is information (no fake 01 / 02 / 03 unless sequential).
- Choose one real aesthetic risk to stand out.
- Work in two passes: brainstorm plan (colors, type, layout, signature), then critique.
- Copy must be clear, conversational, active, and specific.
