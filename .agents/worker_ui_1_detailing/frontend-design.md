Methodology guidelines loaded from frontend-design:
1. Ground design in a concrete subject (Premium Car Detailing in Attapur, Hyderabad)
2. Design custom, deliberate color palettes, typography systems, and signatures
3. Avoid generic defaults (e.g. warm cream serif, near-black acid-green, newspaper rules)
4. Design a thesis-driven Hero section with a characteristic theme
5. Restraint: spend boldness in one signature element, keep the rest quiet and clean
6. Plain, active-voice, specific written copy (no filler, no generic selling)
7. Mobile-ready, accessible contrast, reduced-motion compliance
