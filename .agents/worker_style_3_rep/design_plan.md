# Design Plan: Contemporary Minimalist Jewellery Stores

This plan outlines the visual identity, typography, color palettes, and signature elements for Sri Jagdamba Pearls and Suhani Pittie. Both brands conform to the Cream/Bronze Contemporary Minimalist system but express it through distinct design signatures.

---

## 1. Sri Jagdamba Pearls: Pristine Ivory & Heritage Lustre

### Visual Identity & Tone
Pristine luxury, timeless pearl craftsmanship, and legacy. The design is structured, aligned, and elegant, using thin horizontal and vertical bronze borders that recall classical architectural frames.

### Design Tokens
- **Backgrounds**: `#FDFBF7` (Ivory Cream), `#F7F4EF` (Muted Warm Surface)
- **Typography Text**: `#231F1C` (Deep Charcoal Bronze)
- **Bronze Accents**: `#B58A55` (Rich Heritage Bronze), `#C8A275` (Champagne Gold)
- **Borders**: `#EAE3D5` (Soft Limestone Bronze)
- **Display Font**: `Prata` (Google Font: Serif) - Classic Didone-style luxury
- **Body Font**: `Inter` (Google Font: Sans-serif) - Neutral geometric grounding

### Signature Element
- **The Pearl Luster Customizer**: A custom SVG rendering a classic necklace string where changing the metal type adjusts the clasp gradient and changing the gemstone adjusts the glow and colour properties of the pearl beads (basra white, ruby red, emerald green, diamond shimmer).

## 2. Suhani Pittie Banjara Hills: Avant-Garde Raw Geometry

### Visual Identity & Tone
Artistic, avant-garde, raw hand-beaten metal craftsmanship. Layout is asymmetrical, utilizing overlapping panels, offset borders, and raw structural lines.

### Design Tokens
- **Backgrounds**: `#FAF6F0` (Sandy Warm Cream), `#F1EBE0` (Patinated Soft Neutral)
- **Typography Text**: `#1C1816` (Raw Obsidian Bronze)
- **Bronze Accents**: `#96724E` (Deep Patinated Bronze), `#C49C72` (Burnished Bronze Gold)
- **Borders**: `#DFD3C3` (Muted Bronze Clay)
- **Display Font**: `Cinzel` (Google Font: Serif) - Sculpted roman capital aesthetic
- **Body Font**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Google Font: Sans-serif) - Wider, contemporary modern feel

### Signature Element
- **The Sculptural Wireframe Customizer**: An SVG canvas showing a hand-crafted geometric metal earring. Selecting gold, platinum, or rose gold alters the stroke/fill gradients of the complex overlapping metal sheets, and selected gemstones are rendered as structural rivets in the earring geometry.
