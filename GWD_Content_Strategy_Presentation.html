The Core Mindset Shift

Stop asking: "What if they don't reply to my WhatsApp message?"

Start asking: "How do I use this demo to get 10,000 other people to see my work?"

Old Model (Direct Sales)

New Model (Media Company)

Build 1 site → Record the process → Post everywhere. The 1 owner might ignore you, but 10,000 others watch the video.

The Video Automation Factory

To scale internationally, you cannot manually record 100 videos a day. We automate the content creation alongside the site creation.

  • 1. Auto-Screen Recording (Puppeteer)

    A script automatically opens your deployed `vercel.app/client/[slug]` URL, scrolls down smoothly, and saves a 15-second HD MP4 video.

  • 2. AI Voiceover (ElevenLabs)

    Generate dynamic audio: "Hey [Founder Name], my team built this custom dashboard for [Clinic Name]..."

  • 3. Programmatic Assembly (FFmpeg/Remotion)

    Stitch the scrolling video, the AI voiceover, and a generic video of you pointing (green screen) into a final TikTok/Reel.
