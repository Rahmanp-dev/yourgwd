WE DO NOT HIDE THE STRUCTURE. WE CELEBRATE IT.

At Metal & More Interiors, we build for people who respect structural integrity. Founded in Hyderabad, we reject synthetic finishes and wood substitutes. We deal strictly in raw elements: cold-rolled structural iron, structural mesh plates, copper conduits, and solid concrete poured in-situ.

Our approach is deeply influenced by Neo-Brutalism: letting structural bolts remain visible, showcasing the seams where two metal profiles fuse, and letting the raw texture of sand and aggregate dictate the surfaces. We design lofts, micro-breweries, gyms, and modern industrial residences.

VISIBLE WELD SEAMS

We grind welds smooth for tactile safety, but leave the heat colors intact as an indicator of hand craftsmanship.

POLYURETHANE SEAL

Every raw metal sheet is treated chemically to prevent scaling rust, then sealed under matte industrial finishes.

THE FABRICATION PROTOCOL

Our execution roadmap follows rigorous industrial standards. No shortcuts, strictly monitored.

[01]
METALLURGIC SCHEMATICS

Load Calculations & CAD Drawings

We draft raw engineering layouts specifying iron thickness, weld-seam lengths, and anchor points. No fluff; strictly structural.

[02]
FABRICATION & WELDING

MIG/TIG Fusion & Plaster Casts

Custom metal profiles are cut, ground, and fused in our industrial workshop. Concrete molds are built with heavy timber formwork.

[03]
ANCHORING & LOAD-TEST

Grinding, Treatment & Structural Fitting

We anchor assemblies directly into masonry using chemical Hilti bolts. Weld surfaces are ground flat, leaving raw heat discolors exposed.
