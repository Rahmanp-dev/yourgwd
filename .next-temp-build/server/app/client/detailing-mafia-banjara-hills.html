Detalled Answers

Ceramic Coating is a liquid nano-glass polymer that bonds chemically with your clear coat to provide hydrophobic self-cleaning, chemical resistance, and intense gloss. Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a physical, 200-micron thick elastic urethane wrap that protects against rock chips, deep road scratches, key marks, and possesses self-healing capabilities.
