Principal Architect

S. Vijay Kumar

Vijay Kumar is a graduate of structural architecture, dedicating nearly two decades to designing high-performance residential spaces and signature offices. His philosophy centers around the concept of 'Structural Truth' — allowing raw materials to speak for themselves rather than hiding them under heavy layers.

“Architecture is not just about decorating facades; it is the curation of light, flow, and raw material durability. We bring industrial loft sensibilities to Hyderabad, adapting them to local climate realities.”
