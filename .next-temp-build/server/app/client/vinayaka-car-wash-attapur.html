THE 4-STAGE RESTORATION WORKFLOW

STAGE 01

Decontamination

A dual-stage wash followed by a complete clay bar treatment to remove chemical fallout, tar, and iron particles embedded in the clear coat.

STAGE 02

Paint Correction

Precision machine compounding and polishing to eliminate swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation, restoring optical clarity to the paint.

STAGE 03

Surface Preparation

An isopropyl alcohol (IPA) wipe-down to strip polishing oils and ensure a completely bare surface for maximum ceramic bonding.

STAGE 04

Coating Application

Hand-application of premium 9H nano-ceramic coating, curated under temperature-controlled infra-red curing lamps.
